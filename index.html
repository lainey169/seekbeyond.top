Monster Survivors

Survive endless waves of monsters, upgrade your abilities, and become the ultimate survivor.

Game Overview

Monster Survivors is an action-packed survival game where you must battle against endless waves of monsters. Choose your character, upgrade your abilities, and see how long you can survive in this challenging roguelike experience.

How to Play

Controls

  • WASD or Arrow Keys: Move your character
  • Mouse: Aim your attacks
  • Space/Click: Special ability (when available)
  • E: Pick up items

Tips

  • Keep moving to avoid being surrounded
  • Prioritize upgrading your favorite abilities
  • Collect experience gems to level up faster
  • Health pickups appear randomly - grab them when you can!

Game Features

Multiple Characters

Choose from different characters, each with unique starting abilities and playstyles.

Powerful Upgrades

Collect experience and level up to unlock new abilities or enhance existing ones.

Endless Challenge

Face increasingly difficult waves of monsters as you progress further into the game.

About the Game

Monster Survivors draws inspiration from popular roguelike survival games, offering fast-paced action and addictive gameplay. With procedurally generated enemy waves and a wide variety of upgrade paths, every playthrough provides a unique experience.

The game features stunning pixel art graphics, atmospheric sound design, and intuitive controls that make it accessible to players of all skill levels while still offering a significant challenge to master.
